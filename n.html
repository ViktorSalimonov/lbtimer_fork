LBTimer is installed

You have successfully installed LBTimer. You can now see the LBTimer icon on the right side of the address bar. Click on that icon to access this main panel, view your work of the day, and quickly manage some automatic features.

To access other functions, hover over the big LBTimer icon on the top left of this page.


The timer and task counter will work automatically as soon as you enter the Raterhub page, and all the data will be recorded securely in a hidden folder in your Google Drive™. Most of the functions and buttons of LBTimer are self-explanatory: if you place the mouse cursor on them, a tooltip will appear with an explanation.

This extension is continually under development and has no warranty.

If you have any comment, suggestion or feedback, feel free to contact me.
